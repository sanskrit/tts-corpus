<details><summary>गङ्गानथ-मूलानुवादः</summary>

I am going to explain, in due order, the particular punishments for him who should give false evidence from any one of these causes.—(119)
</details>

<details><summary>मेधातिथिः</summary>

(अग्रे व्याख्यानम्।)
</details>

<details><summary>गङ्गानथ-भाष्यानुवादः</summary>

The construction to be adopted in the following verse should be—‘He who
tells a lie through greed should be fined one thousand’ and so
forth.—(119)
</details>

<details><summary>गङ्गानथ-टिप्पन्यः</summary>

This verse is quoted in *Parāśaramādhava*, (Vyavahāra, p. 82);—and in
*Kṛtyakalpataru* (37a).
</details>
